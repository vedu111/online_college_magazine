About Us - TCET

Our Story

Welcome to Thakur College of Engineering & Technology (TCET), an autonomous and linguistic minority institute. Established in AY 2001-02, our mission is to provide quality technical education in tune with international standards and contemporary global requirements. TCET offers 11 U.G., 3 PG., and 3 Ph.D. (Tech.) programmes. We are proud to be an ISO 9001:2015 certified institute, committed to a student-centric process-driven system for quality education in the 21st century.

Accreditations and Awards

TCET is accredited by NAAC with an "A" grade, and all eligible programs are accredited by NBA for three years. We are the recipients of the IMC - Ramkrishna Bajaj National Quality Commendation Certification 2012 (Education) and the IMC - Ramkrishna Bajaj National Quality Award 2015 (Education). In 2016, TCET was selected for the Asia Pacific Quality Award (APQA) in the education category from Global Pacific Quality Award (GPQA). We have also been recognized as an "Excellent Performing & Innovative Engineering Institute in Maharashtra" at the National Maharashtra Education Summit & Awards 2016.

Institute Highlights

  • Established in A.Y. 2001-02 to improve quality education in Engineering & Technology.
  • Approved by All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), Government of Maharashtra (GoM) & Directorate of Technical Education (DTE).
  • Linguistic (Hindi) speaking minority institute since inception.
  • State of the art infrastructure with a conducive working and learning environment.
  • Active R&D and Industry-Institute Interaction programs for growth, development, and sustainability.
  • 100% assistance for professional placement, including campus placement, higher studies, self-employment, and entrepreneurship.
